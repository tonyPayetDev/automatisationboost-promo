# AutomationBoost Promo — Voice-Over Script

**Language:** French  
**Voice character:** Female, confident, direct, energetic but economical  
**Delivery register:** Mid-tempo, Apple keynote economy of words; silence between sentences is a feature; each sentence punches  
**Tone:** Speaking to creators who know the pain. No fluff. Solution-oriented.

---

"Vous perdez dix heures par semaine à gérer vos réseaux sociaux."

## Delivery Notes

### Pace
- Beats 1–2 are fast (staccato problem statements)
- Beat 3 is mid-tempo (the moment of solution; let it breathe)
- Beat 4 is steady (three stats, each gets equal weight)
- Beat 5 is warm and direct (the ask, delivered with confidence, not pressure)

### Emphasis
- **Beat 1:** "dix heures" — emphasize the time loss
- **Beat 2:** "Trop" — repeat for impact (three times), short hard consonant
- **Beat 3:** "automatiquement" — the magic word, let it land
- **Beat 4:** Numbers (10, 3, 1) — clear and distinct, no mumbling
- **Beat 5:** "Transformez" — the growth word, slightly higher energy

### Silence
- After "Vous perdez dix heures par semaine" → 1s silence while icons enter
- After "Trop de temps perdu" → brief pause before reveal
- Between stat cards → brief pauses to let each card anchor
- After the final CTA → hold the button image in silence with music swell
